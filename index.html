Kronkelschrift

An ornamental way of encoding text, by Victor Voermans, 2021.

Text

Kronkelschrift isn't designed to write very long texts; mostly just names, phrases, or maybe a short poem. The script also can't write spaces, but they aren't as necessary as you might think! Many writing systems around the world do without them. In total, Kronkelschrift can encrypt 33 = 27 characters: the letters a-z, and a comma, which you can use to separate words, phrases, or the lines of a poem. For numbers, you might use Roman numerals.
